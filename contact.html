Adopt or Sponsor a Koala

While you cannot own a koala as a pet, you can participate in adoption and sponsorship programs that help protect these endangered animals.

By sponsoring a koala, you contribute to conservation efforts and habitat protection. Your support makes a real difference in the lives of these remarkable creatures.

How Adoption Programs Work

Our adoption programs allow you to:

  • Sponsor a specific koala in a wildlife sanctuary
  • Receive updates about your adopted koala
  • Help fund conservation and research initiatives
  • Support habitat restoration projects
  • Join a community of koala lovers and advocates

Get Involved Today

Adoption packages start at just $25 per month. Every dollar goes directly to koala conservation efforts.

Contact us to learn more about our adoption programs and how you can help save the koalas!
